Exquisite Enterprises focuses on the premium accessories and luxury goods segment, crafting leather products that meet the exacting standards of international fashion houses and retailers.

Every piece is crafted with meticulous attention to detail, using the finest leathers sourced from the group's own tanneries at Gee & Gee Industries.
