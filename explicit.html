About The Group

A Legacy of
Leather Mastery

Explicit Leather Group is a leading leather goods and garments manufacturing firm, operating as a Recognised Export House under the Council of Leather Exports, Government of India.

Pioneers of the "used", "oiled", and "retro" looks in leather fashion — Explicit has defined global leather trends for over three decades, with collections that travel to buyers across the world every season.
